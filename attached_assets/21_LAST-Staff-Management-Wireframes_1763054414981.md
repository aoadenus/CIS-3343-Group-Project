## Data Source

\`\`\`sql
SELECT 
  e.EmployeeID,
  e.Name,
  e.Role,
  COUNT(o.OrderID) as OrdersProcessed,
  AVG(EXTRACT(HOUR FROM (o.CompletedTime - o.StartTime))) as AvgProcessingTime,
  COUNT(CASE WHEN o.OrderStatus = 'Completed' THEN 1 END) / 
    COUNT(o.OrderID) as CompletionRate,
  AVG(r.QualityScore) as AverageQualityScore,
  SUM(r.Satisfaction) / COUNT(r.ReviewID) as CustomerSatisfaction
FROM employees e
LEFT JOIN orders o ON e.EmployeeID = o.AssignedTo
LEFT JOIN reviews r ON o.OrderID = r.OrderID
WHERE WEEK(o.OrderedDate) = WEEK(CURDATE())
GROUP BY e.EmployeeID, e.Name, e.Role
ORDER BY e.Role, CompletionRate DESC

|------|----------------|----------|

| **On-Call** | Standby, 2-hour notice | Standard |
| **Overtime** | Beyond 40 hours/week | 1.5x pay |
| **Holiday** | Holiday schedule | 2x pay |
| **Training** | New employee onboarding | Standard |

## Automation Features

- **Smart Fill:** Recommends staff based on availability & skills
- **Conflict Detection:** Alerts if staff scheduled twice
- **Break Reminders:** Ensures breaks scheduled per labor laws
- **Payroll Integration:** Auto-calculates hours & estimates pay
- **Notification Queue:** Auto-sends shift confirmations

- **Archive Logs:** 1 year (searchable, summary view)
- **Long-term Archive:** 2 years (searchable, minimal detail)
- **Deletion:** After 2 years, unless compliance hold applied
