## REPORT 6: STAFF PERFORMANCE & INVENTORY
**Access:** Manager ONLY  
**Route:** `/staff/reports/performance`
**Purpose:** Team workload, utilization, available inventory

### Features:

**Part A: Staff Workload:**
- Table:
  - Staff name & role
  - Orders assigned (this week)
  - Orders completed (this week)
  - Avg time per order
  - Quality rating (avg)
  - Utilization % (assigned/capacity)
- Charts:
  - Bar chart: Orders by staff
  - Heatmap: Workload distribution

**Part B: Inventory:**
- Available ingredients:
  - Flavors (chocolate, vanilla, etc.)
  - Fillings (buttercream, mousse, etc.)
  - Icings & colors
  - Cake sizes available
- Stock levels (low/medium/high)
- Reorder alerts (if <5 available)

**Part C: Alerts:**
- Staff with >80% utilization (overloaded)
- Inventory items running low
- Quality issues flagged
